Harry Potter

The main idea

Harry Potter is an exciting, adventure filled series about a "regular" boy who has no parents and a lightning scar on his forehead, One day letters start coming that are addressed to him. No matter where he goes, the letters follow him. When he finally gets one of the letters, he finds out he's a wizard and he's been accepted into Hogwart's School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He ends up being escorted by a giant to the train which will take him to the school. On the train he meets new friends and face some of his biggest fears at school. This series has 7 books, 8 movies and a video game each bringing a brand new adventure.
